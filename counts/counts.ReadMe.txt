Counts of reference backgroundsequences and variants

The notation for a variant consists of the five reference bases followed by the alternate base which substitutes the central base.

So CGTCGA means CG[T>A]CG.

Transcripts of protein coding genes are referred to as coding and RNA genes as nonCoding.
Counts are separated for genes coded on plus and minus strands.
All backgrounds and variants are described as being on the plus strand.
